Day 3 of LaunchWeek brings you one of our most requested features: Document-based Quizzes! Simply upload any PDF, Word document, or text file, and IntelliQ will automatically generate quiz questions based on the content.

Upload your documents to the Document Library and instantly create quizzes from them

How Document-based Quizzes Work:

  1. Upload your document to the Document Library
  2. IntelliQ analyzes the content and generates relevant questions
  3. Customize your quiz settings (number of questions, passing score, etc.)
  4. Start the quiz and test your knowledge of the document
  5. Review your results and identify areas for improvement
